# Failure Analysis

[AgentSkills][PROMPT][START] @failure-analysis

Use after a gate, test, review, hook, or reviewer failure. Analyze before making another change.

Do not modify code, tests, staging, configuration, or expectations. Do not perform consecutive speculative fixes.

Report:

1. Exact failure and command or component.
2. Direct evidence.
3. Plausible causes.
4. Most likely cause and why.
5. Smallest proposed correction.
6. Verification after correction.
7. Whether user permission is required before proceeding.
